This is a project to bring decision making and the decision made to the stakeholders in a transparent and efficient manner.
New decisions can be added by a user with a certain power and delegated above to the upper authority , the upper authority can be a single user or multiple users who are the decision makers based on which the decision is made.
This is can initially prototyped for a songle user for their day to day decisions and a free limited version for a family , a couple or a small start-up.
Decisions can be made visible based on the power level of the user  and the decision makers.
Decisions which are final should be signed off by decision makers and should be visible to all the stakeholders.
the initial decisions visible to the used should be priority basis or day and time basis.
Eisenhower Matrix can be an underlaying factor for decision making process
decisions should have comments so that individuals can put in new perspectives and ideas to the decision making process.
it should also be possible to have a flat heirarical structure and decision making capabilities within and signing off on these.
